Common Questions

About Strategy & Roadmap.

How is this different from the AI Audit?

The Audit surveys your whole business to find AI opportunities. Strategy & Roadmap focuses on planning one specific initiative you've already identified. Audit = "where could AI help?" Strategy = "how do we build this thing right?"

Do I need to do the Audit first?

No. If you already know what you want (a chatbot, an automation, a voice system), you can skip straight to Strategy & Roadmap. The Audit is for businesses still exploring possibilities.

Can I use this plan with another vendor?

Absolutely. The roadmap is yours. Many clients use it to evaluate other vendors, hand it to an existing IT contractor, or even build it themselves if they have the skills. We're not holding it hostage.

What if my project is bigger than one initiative?

Then we should talk about an AI Audit instead. The Audit is built for multi-initiative planning across a business. Strategy & Roadmap is laser-focused on one thing.

Do you give vendor recommendations?

Yes — usually 3 to 5 options compared honestly, including the trade-offs. I'm not on any vendor's payroll, so the recommendation is based on what fits your business, not who pays me.

What does "Q&A for 14 days" actually mean?

Email me any questions about the plan for two weeks after the walkthrough. Clarifications, follow-ups, "what if we changed this?" — all included. Beyond that window, you can move to a Retainer or just book a follow-up consult.
