Before You Book

Common questions, answered upfront.

Are you going to try to sell me something?

No. Discovery Calls are free and end with a recommendation — which is sometimes "you don't need anything from me right now." If AI is the right move and we're a good fit, I'll tell you about the AI Audit (the next paid step). But there's never a hard close on a Discovery Call.

Do I need to prepare anything?

Almost nothing. Show up with a rough sense of what your business does, what's frustrating you, and what you'd want AI to help with (even if you have no idea). That's it. No homework.

What if I have no idea what I want AI to do?

That's the most common starting point — and a great one. Part of my job on the call is to help you see possibilities you didn't know existed. You don't need to know what you want. You just need to know your business.

I'm not technical. Will I understand any of this?

Yes. Speaking plain English about AI is a core part of how I work. If I ever slip into jargon, call me on it. I'd rather over-explain than make you feel lost.

Can I bring my business partner or a team member?

Please do. Decisions are easier when more than one person is in the room. Just let me know who's joining when you book.

What if I'm not ready to spend money on AI?

That's perfectly fine. The call is free and there's no pressure. If timing isn't right, I'll often suggest free or low-cost things you can do on your own. Then if you want to circle back later, you know where to find me.

What happens after the call?

You'll get a short follow-up email summarizing what we discussed and the recommended next step (if any). If you want to move forward with an Audit or another engagement, we'll schedule it from there. If not, no hard feelings.

How quickly can we get on the calendar?

Usually within a few days. Once you submit the form, I'll send you a few time options the same day.
