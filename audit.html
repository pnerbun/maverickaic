Common Questions

About the Audit.

What if AI isn't right for my business yet?

Then we'll tell you. The Audit is honest — if there's no clear opportunity, you'll hear that, and we'll point you toward what to focus on instead. You still walk away with a clear picture.

Do I have to buy a build project after the Audit?

No. The Audit stands on its own. Many clients take the report and run with it themselves, share it with their existing IT person, or use it to evaluate other vendors. If you want us to build, that's a separate conversation.

What information do I need to provide?

For the Quick Scan, we'll ask for a few key documents (your website, a list of your main tools, a description of your top pain points). For the Strategy Audit, add a few short stakeholder interviews. We make it easy — most prep is under an hour for you.

Is my business data safe?

Yes. We sign a mutual NDA before any audit begins, store all materials in a private, encrypted workspace, and delete everything after delivery if you ask us to.

Can I do an on-site audit?

The Strategy Audit can be done on-site at no extra cost if you're within 20 miles of Rockwall, TX. Outside that radius, we can travel for a small fee — just ask during your Discovery Call.

How quickly can we start?

Usually within a week of your Discovery Call. Quick Scans are delivered in 1 week from kickoff. Strategy Audits take 2–3 weeks.

What's NOT included?

The Audit is strategy and direction. It does not include implementation, build work, software costs (tools, subscriptions), or ongoing support. Those are separate engagements we can scope after the Audit if you want our help executing.
