Common Questions

About Retainers.

Do I have to be a build client to get a retainer?

Usually yes. Retainers are designed to support AI tools we built — or that we audited and know inside out. If you have AI you built yourself or with another vendor, we can do an Audit first to get familiar before starting a retainer.

What if I don't use all my hours?

They roll over for 30 days. After that, they expire. We'd rather you use them than save them — and we'll proactively suggest things to do with leftover hours if you don't have ideas.

What if I need MORE than my plan covers?

You can buy extra hours at retainer rates ($150/hr on Care, $125/hr on Growth, $100/hr on Partner) or upgrade your plan mid-month — we'll prorate it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans are month-to-month. No contracts, no cancellation fees. We hand off documentation if you ever want to take things in-house or to another vendor.

What counts as "improvements" in the Growth plan?

Anything that makes your AI work better — adding new features, training it on new content, improving accuracy, adding new integrations, expanding to new channels. If you're not sure, just ask.

What's the "strategic advisory" in the Partner plan?

I act as a part-time AI advisor for your business. New tool came out? I'll evaluate it. Considering a new initiative? We'll talk through it. Vendor pitching you? I'll vet them. It's like having a fractional CTO for AI questions specifically.

How quickly can a retainer start?

Same week, in most cases. For build clients, retainers start the day after your 30-day free post-launch support ends.
