Document Types Explained

Primary Resume

Your best current resume. The AI starts here every run. Tailor it for the broadest range of jobs you target — the AI adapts it to each specific role.

Additional CV

Extra resumes (different industries, career pivots, or older versions with different framing). The scorer picks the best starting point for each job automatically.

Cover Letter Example

Your best past cover letters. Used as style references so the AI matches your natural writing voice — sentence structure, tone, vocabulary.

Context Document

Supplemental context like an internal job description, a company overview, or performance reviews. Injected as background context into every run.

LinkedIn Profile (PDF export)

Optional. Export your LinkedIn profile as a PDF (profile page → "More" → "Save to PDF") and upload it here for a fuller picture of your experience and skills.
